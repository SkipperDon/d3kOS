What d3kOS Does

Everything your chartplotter does.
Plus AI. Plus open source.

🧭
GPS & Navigation

Real-time GPS tracking, route logging, voyage history. OpenSeaMap nautical chart overlay. Works fully offline.

⚙️
Engine Monitoring

NMEA 2000 via CX5106 gateway. RPM, temperature, fuel, battery voltage — colour-coded gauges, live alerts.

🤖
AI Diagnostics

Helm AI analyses your engine data, flags anomalies before they become failures, and explains what it found in plain English.

🎙️
Voice Commands

Hands-free control at the helm. Ask for RPM, speed, heading, or trigger a waypoint without touching the screen.

📡
Cloud Dashboard

T1 (free) connects to atmyboat.com. View your boat's status, voyage logs, and alerts from any browser.

🔒
T0 — 100% Private

Run in T0 mode and d3kOS never phones home. No account. No tracking. No cloud. A core legal and brand promise.
