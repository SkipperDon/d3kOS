What is d3kOS?

d3kOS (Deck OS) is an open-source marine helm control system that brings modern technology to your boat. Think of it as a "smart home" for your vessel‚Äîcentralized monitoring, voice commands, automated logging, and intelligent alerts‚Äîall through a sunlight-readable touchscreen.

Built on Raspberry Pi 4, d3kOS gives boat owners professional-grade engine monitoring, navigation assistance, and AI-powered voice control‚Äîwithout the enterprise price tag.

Perfect for recreational boaters, liveaboards, and marine electronics enthusiasts who want more control, better data, and smarter automation.
