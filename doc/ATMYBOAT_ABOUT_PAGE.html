About At My Boat

Bridging Proprietary Marine Systems with Open Source Innovation

Our Story

At My Boat began in November 2025 with a simple mission: to make marine electronics accessible, affordable, and open to every boater. What started as a personal project by Skipper Don on the waters of Lake Erie has rapidly grown into a trusted resource for boaters seeking to modernize their vessels without breaking the bank.

We believe that marine electronics shouldn't be a mystery. For too long, boaters have been locked into expensive proprietary systems with limited flexibility. At My Boat challenges this status quo by bridging the gap between proprietary marine systems and open-source innovation.

What We Do

At My Boat is your source for:

  • d3kOS Marine Electronics Platform: Our flagship open-source helm control system that brings enterprise-grade monitoring to recreational boaters at a fraction of the cost.
  • DIY Marine Electronics Guides: Step-by-step tutorials for building and installing your own marine systems using affordable, off-the-shelf components.
  • Real-World Testing: Every product and system we recommend has been tested on the water in real Canadian boating conditions.
  • Community Support: Join a growing community of boaters who share knowledge, tips, and troubleshooting advice.

Our Mission

We're here to democratize marine electronics by:

  • Making professional-grade marine monitoring accessible to every boater
  • Providing open-source alternatives to expensive proprietary systems
  • Creating compatibility bridges between old and new marine technology
  • Sharing knowledge freely through our blog, guides, and community

Why d3kOS?

The d3kOS (d3k Operating System) is our answer to the marine electronics challenge. Built on proven Raspberry Pi hardware and open-source software, d3kOS provides:

  • NMEA2000 Integration: Read data from your existing marine sensors and chartplotters
  • Voice Assistant: Hands-free control and monitoring while you're at the helm
  • Marine Vision Camera: AI-powered fish detection and forward collision watch
  • Cloud Sync: Access your boat data from anywhere via mobile app
  • Affordable: Complete system for under $1,000 vs. $5,000+ proprietary alternatives

Rapid Growth

Since launching in November 2025, At My Boat has experienced rapid growth within the marine electronics community. Our blog posts reach thousands of boaters monthly, and our d3kOS system is being adopted by recreational boaters, fishing enthusiasts, and even small commercial operators across North America.

What sets us apart is our commitment to real-world practicality. Every feature in d3kOS was born from actual needs encountered on the water. We don't build technology for technology's sake—we solve real problems boaters face every day.
